## Introduction
The **International Monetary Fund (IMF)** is an international financial institution created to promote global economic stability and cooperation. It provides financial assistance, policy advice, and technical support to its member countries.

## History / Origins
- Established in **July 1944** at the **Bretton Woods Conference** in New Hampshire, USA.  
- Officially came into existence in **December 1945**, with 29 founding member countries.  
- Created alongside the World Bank to rebuild the global economy after World War II.

  - Provide resources to countries facing balance of payments difficulties.  
  - Support poverty reduction and sustainable economic growth.
