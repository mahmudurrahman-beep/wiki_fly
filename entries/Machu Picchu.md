## History / Origins
- Built around **1450 CE** during the reign of Inca emperor **Pachacuti**.  
- Served as a royal estate and religious site for the Inca elite.  
- Abandoned in the 16th century after the Spanish conquest, though never discovered by the conquistadors.  
- Rediscovered for the outside world in **1911** by American explorer **Hiram Bingham**.

- **Design**: Reflects advanced Inca engineering, blending with the natural landscape.  

## Legacy / Impact
