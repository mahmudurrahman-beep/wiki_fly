# Evolution of Computers

A concise history of computing from ancient tools to modern digital systems.

## Early Devices
- **Abacus** and mechanical calculators; conceptual work by **Charles Babbage**.

## Generations
1. **First (1940s–1950s):** Vacuum tubes (e.g., ENIAC).  
2. **Second (1950s–1960s):** Transistors.  
3. **Third (1960s–1970s):** Integrated circuits.  
4. **Fourth (1970s–1980s):** Microprocessors and personal computers.  
5. **Fifth (1980s–present):** AI, parallel processing, networking, mobile computing.

## Modern Era
- Internet, smartphones, cloud computing, and emerging fields like **quantum computing** and advanced AI.
