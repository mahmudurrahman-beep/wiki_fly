# Chinese Revolution (1911–1949)

A period of political upheaval that ended imperial rule and led to the founding of the People’s Republic of China.

## Key Phases
- **1911 Revolution:** Overthrow of the Qing dynasty; Republic of China established.  
- **Warlord Era:** Fragmentation and struggle for national unity.  
- **Rise of CCP:** Chinese Communist Party founded (1921); Long March (1934–1935).  
- **Japanese invasion (1937–1945):** Temporary Nationalist-Communist cooperation.  
- **Civil War:** Resumed after WWII; **1949** Communist victory and PRC proclaimed; Nationalists retreat to Taiwan.

## Significance
- Ended dynastic rule; reshaped China’s political and social order; set the stage for modern Chinese state.
