William Henry Gates III (born October 28, 1955) is an American business magnate, software developer, philanthropist, and co‑founder of Microsoft Corporation. He is widely recognized as one of the most influential figures in the history of technology.

## Early Life

- Born in Seattle, Washington, USA.  
- Son of William H. Gates Sr. and Mary Maxwell Gates.  
- Demonstrated early interest in computers and programming.  
- Attended Harvard University but left in 1975 to pursue software development.  

## Career

### Founding of Microsoft
- Co‑founded **Microsoft** with Paul Allen in 1975.  
- Developed MS‑DOS, which became the foundation for IBM PCs.  
- Launched **Windows** in 1985, revolutionizing personal computing with a graphical interface.  

### Growth and Leadership
- Served as Microsoft’s CEO until 2000.  
- Oversaw expansion into operating systems, office software, and internet technologies.  
- Microsoft became the world’s largest software company.  

### Philanthropy
- Co‑founded the **Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation** in 2000.  
- Focus areas: global health, education, poverty reduction, and climate change.  
- Major initiatives include vaccination programs, malaria eradication, and clean energy research.  

## Personal Life

- Married Melinda French in 1994 (divorced in 2021).  
- Three children.  
- Continues philanthropic work through the Gates Foundation.  

## Wealth and Recognition

- Consistently ranked among the world’s richest individuals.  
- Net worth has exceeded $100 billion.  
- Recognized for contributions to technology and philanthropy.  
- Awarded numerous honors, including honorary doctorates and the Presidential Medal of Freedom.  

## Legacy

* Revolutionized personal computing through Microsoft.  
* Pioneered software licensing and business models.  
* Global leader in philanthropy, focusing on health and education.  
* Symbol of innovation, entrepreneurship, and social responsibility.  

## Key Dates

- **1955** – Born in Seattle, Washington  
- **1975** – Co‑founded Microsoft  
- **1985** – Launch of Microsoft Windows  
- **2000** – Founded Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation  
- **2021** – Divorce from Melinda Gates
