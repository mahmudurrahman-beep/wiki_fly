## Introduction
The **International Cricket Council (ICC)** is the world governing body for cricket. It manages international competitions, sets rules, and promotes the sport globally.

## History / Origins
- Founded in **1909** as the **Imperial Cricket Conference** by England, Australia, and South Africa.  
- Renamed the **International Cricket Conference** in 1965.  
- Became the **International Cricket Council** in 1989, reflecting its global role.
