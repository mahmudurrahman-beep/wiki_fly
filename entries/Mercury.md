## Introduction
Mercury is the smallest planet in the Solar System and the closest to the Sun. It has a rocky surface, extreme temperature variations, and no significant atmosphere.

## History / Origins
- Known since ancient times; visible to the naked eye.  
- Named after **Mercury**, the Roman messenger god.  
- Studied in detail by spacecraft such as **Mariner 10** and **MESSENGER**.  

## Key Features
- Diameter: ~4,879 km.  
- Orbit: Fastest of all planets, completing a year in **88 Earth days**.  
- Atmosphere: Extremely thin, composed of oxygen, sodium, hydrogen, helium, and potassium.
