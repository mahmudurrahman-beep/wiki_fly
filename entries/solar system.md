# Solar System Overview

## Central Star
- **The Sun**: A G-type main-sequence star (G2V).  
- Contains **99.8% of the Solar System’s mass**.  
- Provides energy and gravity that governs planetary orbits.  

## Planets
- **Inner (Terrestrial) Planets**: Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars.  
- **Outer (Gas/Ice Giants)**: Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune.  
- Orbit ranges: Mercury (0.39 AU) → Neptune (30 AU).  

## Dwarf Planets
- Recognized: **Pluto, Ceres, Haumea, Makemake, Eris**.  
- Many more candidates in the **Kuiper Belt** and beyond.  

## Other Bodies
- **Moons**: Over 750 known natural satellites.  
- **Asteroids**: ~1.4 million catalogued.  
- **Comets**: ~4,600 known.  
- **Kuiper Belt & Oort Cloud**: Reservoirs of icy bodies.  

## Location in Galaxy
- Situated in the **Orion–Cygnus Arm** of the Milky Way.  
- Nearest star: **Proxima Centauri (4.24 light-years)**.  
- Takes ~230 million years to orbit the galactic center.
