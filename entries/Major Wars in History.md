## Ancient & Classical
- **Trojan War (c. 1200 BCE)** – Legendary conflict between Greeks and Trojans.  
- **Greco-Persian Wars (492–449 BCE)** – Greece vs. Persia, defining Western civilization’s survival.  
- **Peloponnesian War (431–404 BCE)** – Athens vs. Sparta, reshaped Greek politics.  
- **Punic Wars (264–146 BCE)** – Rome vs. Carthage, secured Roman dominance in the Mediterranean.  

## Medieval
- **Crusades (1096–1291)** – Religious wars between Christians and Muslims over the Holy Land.  
- **Hundred Years’ War (1337–1453)** – England vs. France, influenced nationalism and military tactics.  
- **Mongol Conquests (13th century)** – Largest contiguous empire in history, reshaping Eurasia.  

## Early Modern
- **Thirty Years’ War (1618–1648)** – Religious and political conflict in Europe, devastating Germany.  
- **Napoleonic Wars (1803–1815)** – France vs. European coalitions, spread revolutionary ideals.  
- **American Revolutionary War (1775–1783)** – U.S. independence from Britain.  

## Modern
- **American Civil War (1861–1865)** – Union vs. Confederacy, ended slavery in the U.S.  
- **World War I (1914–1918)** – Global conflict, collapse of empires, League of Nations formed.  
- **World War II (1939–1945)** – Largest war in history, defeat of fascism, creation of the UN.  
- **Cold War Conflicts (1947–1991):**  
  - **Korean War (1950–1953)**  
  - **Vietnam War (1955–1975)**  
  - **Soviet–Afghan War (1979–1989)**  

## Contemporary
- **Gulf War (1990–1991)** – Coalition vs. Iraq after invasion of Kuwait.  
- **Iraq War (2003–2011)** – U.S.-led invasion, regime change.  
- **Afghanistan War (2001–2021)** – Longest U.S. war, Taliban resurgence.  
- **Russia–Ukraine War (2014–present, escalated 2022)** – Largest European conflict since WWII.
