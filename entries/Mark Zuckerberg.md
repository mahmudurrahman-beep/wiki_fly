# Mark Zuckerberg

Mark Elliot Zuckerberg (born May 14, 1984) is an American computer programmer, entrepreneur, and philanthropist. He is best known as the co‑founder and CEO of **Meta Platforms**, the parent company of Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, and other products.

## Early Life and Education

- Born in White Plains, New York, USA.  
- Son of Edward Zuckerberg and Karen Kempner.  
- Attended Harvard University, where he began developing Facebook in 2004.  
- Dropped out of Harvard to focus on building Facebook.  

## Career

1. **Facebook Launch (2004)**  
   - Created Facebook with classmates Eduardo Saverin, Andrew McCollum, Dustin Moskovitz, and Chris Hughes.  
   - Initially limited to Harvard students, later expanded globally.  

2. **Growth and Expansion**  
   - Facebook became the world’s largest social media platform.  
   - Acquired Instagram (2012) and WhatsApp (2014).  
   - Rebranded parent company as **Meta Platforms** in 2021 to focus on the metaverse and AI.  

3. **Philanthropy**  
   - Co‑founded the **Chan Zuckerberg Initiative (CZI)** with his wife, Priscilla Chan, in 2015.  
   - Focuses on science, education, and community development.  
   - In 2025, CZI stopped funding FWD.us, a pro‑immigration advocacy group Zuckerberg had co‑founded.  

## Personal Life

- Married **Priscilla Chan** in 2012.  
- They have three children.  
- Siblings include Randi Zuckerberg and Donna Zuckerberg.  

## Wealth and Recognition

- Ranked among the richest people in the world, with a net worth exceeding $200 billion in 2025.  
- Listed on Forbes’ Billionaires and Powerful People rankings.  
- Known for pledging to donate 99% of his Meta shares over his lifetime through CZI.  

## Recent Developments (2025)

- Meta invested heavily in AI, including a $14 billion stake in Scale AI, bringing Alexandr Wang to lead its AI division.  
- Reports suggest Zuckerberg’s **micromanagement style** has caused tension within Meta’s AI team.  
- CZI shifted focus away from immigration advocacy toward **science and AI research**.  

---

*Mark Zuckerberg remains one of the most influential figures in technology and philanthropy, shaping global communication through Meta and investing in science and AI for the future.*
