# The Quran

The **Quran** is the sacred scripture of **Islam**, believed by Muslims to be the literal word of **God (Allah)** as revealed to the Prophet **Muhammad** through the angel **Gabriel**.

## Structure
- Composed of **114 chapters (surahs)**.  
- Written in **Arabic**; recited and memorized worldwide.

## Themes
- Monotheism, guidance for worship and ethics, law, stories of earlier prophets, and spiritual instruction.

## Significance
- Primary source of Islamic theology, law, and practice; central to prayer and religious life.
