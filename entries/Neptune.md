## Introduction
Neptune is the eighth and farthest planet from the Sun in the Solar System. Classified as an **ice giant**, it is known for its deep blue color caused by methane in its atmosphere. Neptune is similar in composition to Uranus but more active, with powerful storms and high-speed winds.
