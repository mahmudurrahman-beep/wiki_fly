## Early Life
- Born in **Rosario, Argentina**.  
- Joined **Newell’s Old Boys** youth academy before moving to **FC Barcelona** at age 13 due to growth hormone treatment needs.  

## Club Career
- **Barcelona (2004–2021)**:  
  - 520 appearances, 474 goals.  
  - Won **10 La Liga titles**, **4 Champions League titles**, and **7 Copa del Rey trophies**.  
- **Paris Saint‑Germain (2021–2023)**:  
  - 58 appearances, 22 goals.  
  - Won **2 Ligue 1 titles**.  
- **Inter Miami (2023–present)**:  
  - Became the face of MLS, scoring **35 goals in the 2025 season**.  
  - Helped Inter Miami win the **MLS Cup**.  

## International Career
- Debuted for Argentina in **2005**.  
- Achievements:  
  - **FIFA World Cup Winner (2022)**.  
  - **Copa América Winner (2021, 2024)**.  
  - Argentina’s all‑time top scorer with **115 goals**.  

## Awards & Records
- **8 Ballon d’Or titles** (most in history).  
- FIFA World Player of the Year multiple times.  
- All‑time top scorer for Barcelona and La Liga.  
- Holds record for most goals in a calendar year (**91 goals in 2012**).  

## Legacy
- Known for his **dribbling, vision, passing, and finishing**.  
- Revered as a global sports icon, often compared with Pelé and Maradona.  
- Continues to inspire millions worldwide with his humility and excellence.
